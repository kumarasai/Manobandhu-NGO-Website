OUR VISION

Our vision is to eliminate the neglect and discrimination faced by mentally ill individuals, particularly those who wander the streets, and ensure their economic well-being and access to basic human rights. We believe that by offering proper mental health facilities and medication, we can integrate them into society. Our goal is to transform the lives of wandering mentally ill individuals in India. Through collaborative efforts involving concerned citizens, civil society groups, and the government, we aspire to empower them to lead dignified lives and secure their fundamental human rights.

Psychiatrist Ramon Magsaysay Awardee Founder, Shraddha Rehabilitation Foundation, Mumbai
Dr. Bharat Vatwani
Dr. Bharat Vatwani along with his wife Dr. Smita founded 'Shraddha Rehabilitation Foundation ' in 1988. It is a charitable NGO dedicated to the rescue, treatment, rehabilitation and reunification with their families of the wandering mentally ill destitute on the streets of India. Till now more than 9000 recovered patients were reunited with their families across the country.
